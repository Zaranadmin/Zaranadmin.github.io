Services

01.

Asphalt Roofing

We provide a wide range of asphalt works from strip and relay with or without fully bonded insulation ranging from asphalt roofing to asphalt paving.

02.

Slate Roofing

We specialise in stripping and renewing slate roofs, whilst using the latest cutting edge techniques.
We also specifically redo your roofing with high quality products such as the new vent 3 breathable membrane roofing felt and all new tanalised roofing battens.

03.

Sheet Lead Roofing

We provide a wide range of sheet lead roofing ranging from, working with sheet metal roofing on Dormers and Mansards to installing new rain water guttering and downpipes.
All lead works are treated with Pantination Oil.

04.

Tile Roofing

We provide a wide range of tile roofing works ranging from stripping and retiling to providing and installing your roof windows.
